A Hotspot Of Geological Activity



Four Tectonic Plates

Japan lies at the intersection of four tectonic plates:

  • Eurasian Plate
  • Okhotsk Plate
  • Pacific Plate
  • Philippine Plate

These plates are responsible for forming much of the mountainous islands that make up japan. They are also responsible for frequent earthquakes and their resulting tsunamis. However, this has given the region a rich amount of resources. There is a little bit of almost every metal and mineral in the country, including diamonds.

Wide Variety Of Climates

The vertical orientation of the country as well as its many mountains means the climate varies throughout the country. From deserts and frigid alpine areas, to idyllic beaches and temperate rain-forests, there are an astonishing amount of biomes. This, combined with its nature as a collection of isolated islands has let it to be a hotbed of biodiversity. There are a large amount of species found only here.
